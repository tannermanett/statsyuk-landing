Welcome to the NHL Database

Hockey is a game of speed, skill, and strategy—and we believe data can help bring it to the next level. Our platform provides in-depth NHL analytics in a way that’s easy to explore, whether you're a casual watcher, a die-hard fan, or an analyst, looking for an edge. To get started, use the navigation bar on the left side of the screen, and head over to the General Players Page . Be sure to click on your favourite players name and check out the side-bar for their analysis.

What's New

We're on Twitter! Find us at @StatsyukHockey for the latest updates and news on our NHL Database. We'll be posting about new features, and sharing our #Statsyuk Radar Charts for our favourite players.

We've recently added a new feature to our sidebar - the Salary Info. This feature provides a breakdown of a player's salary, including their cap hit, any clauses in their contract, and a detailed breakdown of their base salary over the years. We also recently added the Standings table, and have many more updates coming to it soon! Check it out to see how your team ranks in stats like Goal Differential, Point Percentage, and many more insightful stats.

Coming Soon

Introducing our new Gradient Boost Model for Expected Goals per Shot! This cutting-edge machine learning model leverages advanced gradient boosting techniques to evaluate shot quality by taking into account factors like shot location, player positioning, and game context. It’s designed to deliver a more precise prediction of scoring probabilities, providing deeper insights into player performance and strategic play.

This revamped page will not only display team rankings but also feature interactive charts and graphs that highlight key performance metrics such as goal differential, point percentage, and more.
